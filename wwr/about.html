History

Rafting is an adventure sport (definition imposed by the ministry of sport) that is based on the practice of descending rapids as a team using inflatable boats and safety equipment. Before starting any rafting descent, an activity driver gives all participants detailed safety instructions. These instructions are remembered by other drivers during strategic moments of the descent, and compliance with them is essential for everyone's safety. Commercial rafting provides the experience of going down the river for people of any age and, for the most part, people who have never had a previous experience, making the sport accessible
